Introduction
Analyzing public transportation safety provides a basis for government agencies to carry out policies and evaluate current regulations; it also benefits the general public by increasing their awareness of the dominant factors that closely relate to fatality during public transportation. Possible technical challenges include: 1. Having professional knowledge to understand raw data, 2. Integrating different types of accidents and the causes into one dataset, 3. Different transportation tools should be analyzed through tailored visualizations and analysis, 4. Finding a standard metrics to rank and evaluate different factors.
